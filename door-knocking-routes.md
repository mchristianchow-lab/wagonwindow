## Field Notes

- **Best times to knock:** Tue–Thu 4–7pm · Sat 10am–1pm. Avoid Mon/Fri.
- **Cul-de-sacs first:** Look for the dead-end loops on Google Maps before you leave. They convert fastest — neighbors see you working and flag you down.
- **Pitch:** "Hey, I'm with Wagon Windows — when's the last time you actually had time to do your own windows? We're in the area this week. Takes us 2 hours, off your plate for the season. Want a number?"
- **Leave something:** Business card or flyer with QR code to the quote page. No = maybe in 2 weeks.
- **After every job:** Ask in person — "If you're happy, would you mind leaving us a Google review? Takes 30 seconds." Hand them the card with the review link.
- **48-hour return:** After a job on a street, knock the immediate neighbours the next day — "Just finished next door, want us to do yours while we're in the area?"
