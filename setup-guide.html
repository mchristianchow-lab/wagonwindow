Wagon Windows — Website Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get your site live on wagonwindows.com

Step 1 — Deploy Your Site to Netlify

Action

Go to netlify.com/drop and drag your entire wagonwindows folder onto the page. Your site will be live at a temporary URL within seconds.

Then

You'll be prompted to create a free Netlify account to claim the site. Sign up with your email — no credit card required. Once claimed, the site is yours permanently.

Step 2 — Connect wagonwindows.com

2a

In your Netlify dashboard, go to your site → Site settings → Domain management → Add custom domain

2b

Type wagonwindows.com and click Verify. Netlify will show you 2 DNS records to add.

2c

Log into wherever you bought your domain (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.) and add those 2 DNS records. Not sure how? Reply with where you bought the domain and you'll get step-by-step instructions.

2d

Wait up to a few hours. Your site will be live at wagonwindows.com — Netlify sets up HTTPS (the padlock) automatically for free.

Step 3 — Set Up Your Contact Form

Action

To receive quote requests by email, go to formspree.io → create a free account → create a new form → copy your Form ID (looks like xabcdefg).

Then

Open index.html in a text editor, find YOUR_FORM_ID, and replace it with your Formspree ID. Re-upload the updated file to Netlify by dragging it into your site dashboard.

Step 4 — Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Action

Go to business.google.com and create a free profile for Wagon Windows. This is what shows up when customers search "window cleaning Salmon Arm" on Google Maps.

Then

Once you have your Google Business profile URL, send it over and the "5.0 on Google" badge in your trust bar will be linked directly to your reviews.

⚠️ Ask every happy customer to leave a Google review. Even 5–10 reviews puts you ahead of most local competitors.

Step 5 — Your Calendly Booking is Already Live

✅ Your Calendly booking widget is embedded directly on the site. Customers can book appointments without calling. Make sure your Calendly availability is up to date at calendly.com/davidhanna-wagonwindow

Questions about any of these steps? Reach out and you'll get support.
