## How to get your GBP review link:
1. Search "Wagon Windows" on Google
2. Click "Get more reviews" on your GBP panel
3. Copy the short URL and replace [GBP_REVIEW_LINK] above

## Notes:
- Send within 24h of job completion — response rate drops fast after that
- Text outperforms email 3:1 for review asks
- Don't ask more than once per customer
- Target: 2-3 new reviews per week
