Pre-Existing Damage
Wagon Window Washers is not liable for pre-existing damage to glass, frames, seals, screens, or tracks — including chips, cracks, failed seals, fogging between panes, or paint overspray. We document and flag anything we notice before we start.

Tempered & Heat-Treated Glass
Some tempered or heat-treated glass contains microscopic manufacturing particles embedded in the surface. These can become dislodged during professional cleaning, causing fine scratches — this is an industry-wide condition unrelated to technique. Wagon Window Washers accepts no liability for scratches on tempered or heat-treated glass. Let us know before we start if you'd like us to avoid scrapers on specific panes.

Hard Water Stains
Hard water staining is caused by mineral deposits in water — not by window cleaning. Standard cleaning will not remove them. Stain removal is a separate chemical treatment quoted independently, and results vary by severity. We are not liable for stains remaining after a standard clean.

Screens & Tracks
Screens are handled with care, but we are not liable for screens that are already damaged, loose, or weakened prior to removal — we will flag anything we notice. Track cleaning is a best-effort service; heavy buildup may not fully clear without track replacement, which is outside our scope.
