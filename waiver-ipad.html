Pre-Existing Damage
Wagon Window Washers is not liable for pre-existing damage to glass, frames, seals, screens, or tracks — including chips, cracks, failed seals, fogging between panes, or paint overspray. We document and flag anything we notice before we start.
Tempered & Heat-Treated Glass
Some tempered or heat-treated glass contains microscopic manufacturing particles embedded in the surface. These can become dislodged during professional cleaning, causing fine scratches — this is an industry-wide condition unrelated to technique. Wagon Window Washers accepts no liability for scratches on tempered or heat-treated glass.
Hard Water Stains
Hard water staining is caused by mineral deposits — not by window cleaning. Standard cleaning will not remove them. Stain removal is a separate chemical treatment quoted independently, and results vary by severity. We are not liable for stains remaining after a standard clean.
Screens & Tracks
Screens are handled with care, but we are not liable for screens that are already damaged, loose, or weakened prior to removal. Track cleaning is a best-effort service; heavy buildup may not fully clear without track replacement, which is outside our scope.
Property Access & Ladders
We require clear, safe access to all windows. If a window is not safely reachable we will skip it and advise you. We are not liable for minor incidental contact with gardens or outdoor furniture during normal ladder use.
Interior Access
For interior cleaning, please clear valuables, electronics, and fragile items from windows before we arrive. We are not liable for items left in the work area. Agreeing to interior service grants Wagon Window Washers access to your home solely for the work described above.
Gutter Cleaning
Gutter cleaning includes clearing debris and flushing downspouts. We are not liable for pre-existing gutter damage, separated joints, or blocked underground drainage. If downspouts are blocked or gutters are damaged, we will advise before proceeding.
Satisfaction & Re-Clean Policy
If you're not satisfied, contact us within 24 hours and we'll return to address it at no charge. After 24 hours, or where the issue is caused by weather, re-contamination, or conditions outside our control, we are unable to offer a re-clean under this policy.
Payment
Payment is due on the day of service, collected at the time of waiver signing before work begins. We accept cash or e-transfer (davidhanna@wagonwindow.com). The quoted price is the final price — any scope changes are agreed before we proceed.
Cancellations
Please provide 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule. Late cancellations may incur a fee of up to $50 to cover travel costs. If weather makes the job unsafe, we reschedule at no cost to either party.
Liability Limit
To the fullest extent permitted under BC law, Wagon Window Washers' total liability for any claim is limited to the amount paid for the service. You agree to hold Wagon Window Washers, its owner, and technicians harmless from third-party claims arising from pre-existing property conditions or circumstances outside our direct control.
