## Tips:
- Post every 7 days minimum — Google rewards active profiles
- Always include a photo (posts with photos get 3x more views)
- Include at least one location name (Salmon Arm, Shuswap, etc.) in every post
- Rotate between the 4 CTAs: Book, Get a quote, Learn more, Call now
